Discover Our Authentic Flavors

Biscoff Crumble

Creamy vanilla base infused with rich cookie butter and layered with crunchy Biscoff biscuit pieces. A favorite for cookie lovers.

Tropical Mango

Made only with ripe, sweet mangoes and a touch of lemon. An intensely fruity and refreshing sorbet. 100% Vegan.

Raspberry Dream

Intense and slightly tart flavor, made with pure, locally sourced raspberries. A refreshing choice, naturally low in sugar.

Sweet Cantaloupe

The essence of summer in a scoop. Light, creamy gelato featuring the delicate, aromatic flavor of fresh yellow melon.

Bourbon Vanilla

The Italian classic, elevated. Made with fresh milk and high-quality Bourbon vanilla beans for a rich, aromatic flavor.

Cookies & Cream (Oreo)

Rich vanilla cream base loaded with generous chunks of chocolate sandwich cookies. A deliciously crunchy experience.

Pistachio Royale

Our signature flavor: made with slow-roasted Bronte pistachios from Sicily. Rich, authentic, transports you directly to Italy.

Amarena Swirl

Creamy milk base speckled with decadent, slightly tart Amarena cherries from Italy. A timeless and elegant flavor.

Wild Berry Medley

A vibrant mix of blueberries, blackberries, and strawberries. Intense, natural flavor that captures the taste of the forest.

The Secret of Authentic Italian Taste

Original from Rome: The Secret Whipped Cream

Our whipped cream is not just any cream. It is prepared daily following a family recipe brought directly from Rome. Its fine texture and balanced taste are the perfect complement to every gelato sortiment. We guarantee an authentic experience!

100% Purity: Natural Ingredients and Low Sugar

We are committed to using only 100% natural ingredients, with no artificial flavors or preservatives. Our gelato is made with real fruits and has a low sugar content, allowing the natural flavor of the raw ingredients to shine. Healthy taste, delicious taste!
